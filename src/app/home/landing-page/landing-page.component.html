Easy To Get Started

ArtifactsPro, unlike most other similar solutions do not require, any complex licensing, set up or customization. It is built to be simple and for usability which is aimed at saving time and money and increasing your team/business' effeciency and capabilities.

Revision Control

ArtifactsPro provides a place for you to keep versions a,b,c,d etc. of your documents in one place. See who upload changes, make comments, and search capabilities to identify changes in a few seconds through comprehensive search.

Separate Business Concerns

To allow your business to effectivley operate on ArtifactsPro, you can create virtual divisions or departments that allows multiple teams to exist on the platform, where communication and management of documents are possible within each virtual department or division.

Access Control

Control who specifically has access to to what documents within departments or teams. You can also password protect revision documents, and modify how each user can access a document.

Organization Structure

Reflect the roles individuals in ArtifactsPro. You can modify the permission of each member on the platform, and also in virtual departments to reflect your structure.
